Large BSS and OSS vendors for telecommunication operators normally offer full-scale enterprise product catalog (EPC) solutions which are deeply integrated into their BSS and OSS suites supporting all the vertical product modelling, pricing and provisioning functions. Also some support product life-cycle management (PLM) workflows.

It is common though that in practical telco operator landscapes that kind of solutions are not fully used as they are difficult to implement and support (due to frequent changes in telco products and related product modelling and provisioning & integration requirements changes), not to speak about the fact that they are quite expensive solutions, which makes it difficult to justify them from business case perspective.

Still most of the telecom operators experience the permanent need to use the platform, which can manage structured product information in automated way:

to make product content commonly defined and structured for all target systemsdistribute it automatically to all front- and back-end platforms which deal with product information for customer care and messaging purposesto minimise the manual work of content managers in all those systems to create / update / verify product descriptionshave a single source of the truth for commercial product informationbe able to compare technical product model (product settings implemented on BSS or network platforms) and it's parameters with commercial model (product parameters visible for end-users) and allow to deal with discrepancies.
