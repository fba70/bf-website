Recent decade was almost definitely driven by AI technologies and tools development and finding applications for B2C and B2B purposes. There is no doubt that plenty of them are already on the market with hundreds of different-purpose AI models (working with text, sound, graphics and video content) and thousands of applications and attempts to make the technology available for users.

Despite the number of applications and huge potential to do things which one could not imagine even a few years ago, most of them are mostly relevant for B2C use cases when users can invest their time to work with models, prompts and quality checking of the results. Good examples for that could be AI tools for graphics and video (mostly showing up just recently) manipulation or generation based on prompts.

One may ask - how that could be made part of B2B applications or enterprise workflow. Our answer would be that it is only possible within data-driven processes. It means if you have the data pipelines for your products, marketing campaigns, clients, events, news they can be used to assemble dynamic data-driven and context aware prompts for one or several AI tools. Modern techniques (such as RAG - retrieval augmented generation) allow also to make your broad context (e.g. your company market, technologies, marketing concepts) to be used to augment general purpose AI tools (like LLMs for example).

IN4COM GmbH offers platform **AVICA** ( [https://avica.cloud](https://avica.cloud) ) which implements this concept for **data-driven automated video content production** oriented for B2B / enterprise purposes. You can use your data to automatically create diverse videos using our concept of data-driven dynamic storytelling for many different business domains and use cases.
